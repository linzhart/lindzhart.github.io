Maybe this should be a laundry blog. But a laundry FAIL blog, where you can go to get tips about how to stink at laundry (leave it in the washer overnight - ha). A blog for those people who are so perfect they need to pretend to be bad at stuff so that people will like them.

It is mostly my job to do the laundry, since I am home the most. But Husband helps out too. Because of this I try to keep him informed about what is going on in the laundry sector of my life.

conversation:

"I'm thinking of making laundry soap again. I didn't like the way the last batch turned out but it is so much cheaper. . . . "

two weeks later: "I've been trying to get to the store for laundry soap ingredients. . . "

two weeks later laundry soap ingredients sitting on the kitchen counter in a grocery sack. The sack is removed and they sit for another week.

The bar soap is grated and stored in an old soap container. It joins the other ingredients on the counter.

A week later: "I found a new recipe for laundry soap that I'm going to try, but it calls for so much water I don't have a bucket big enough to mix it in. I'm thinking I'll borrow one from my mom."

A week later the bucket shows up outside our back door where it sits for several days.

I finally get around to making the soap. The whole house smells like soap and the five-gallon-bucket sits on the counter for another four days. Me: "I don't have a funnel"
