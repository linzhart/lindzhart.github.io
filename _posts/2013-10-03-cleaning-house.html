Mommy is genetically a clean-freak, but I'm trying very hard to overcome my obsession in order to make our home more happy, even if it is less clean.

And since there are five of you, most of the time the mess is winning, I'm trying to come to peace with that.  I tell myself - it isn't dirt, just clutter; and if it means that I didn't yell at anyone, the fact that there are leftover blanket forts and Legos all over the living room just means that we've had a good day.

I like to think I'm striking the right balance and that our house is mostly clean most of the time.

Screaming "It's the end of the world as we know it!" the entire time I vacuum is very demoralizing.  Please stop.

Love, Mommy
