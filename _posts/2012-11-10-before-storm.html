This is our house 10 minutes before the boys come home from school.  Everyone content and happy while we blast Weezer from the iPod.  It takes me all morning to get to this moment.  20 minutes later all is chaos and mess and screaming.  I recorded it to remind me that it does exist.  Even if only in 10 minute increments.
