(written by an 8-year-old)


This is a piece of my third grader's "getting to know you" poster for school. It says: "Mom: She cooks dinner, she washes our clothes, she makes babies. She is my mom. My mom loves to read and so do I."

It is sweet, and also a bit sad. I didn't realize my life could be contained in three simple sentences.
 But there it is.
