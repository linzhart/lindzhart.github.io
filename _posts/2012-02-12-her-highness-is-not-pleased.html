--- layout: post title: Her Highness is Not Pleased date: '2012-02-12T21:20:00.000-07:00' author: Lindsay tags: - girls - quotes modified_time: '2012-02-12T21:48:44.046-07:00' thumbnail: http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-RUu4ma7kuHY/TziV7zPG9XI/AAAAAAAAAwI/tfP32o_ZjiU/s72-c/IMGP2671.JPG blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6431381294158046892.post-1647472337278889788 blogger_orig_url: http://inconclusiveexperiences.blogspot.com/2012/02/her-highness-is-not-pleased.html --- It has been unseasonably warm, so we left the front door open for a little while the other day.  Lil' Girl saw the sun streaming into the living room and got very upset.
Her: "Why is the door open?"
John: "Because it is a beautiful day outside."
Her: "Well, I HATE it.  The clouds are not PINK.  It is NOT a beautiful day!"

And after the fourth trip to the bathroom when she was supposed to be sleeping.
Me: "Go to bed and stay there."
Her: *sigh* "Mom, don't worry about ME."
Me: "Stay in bed."
Her: (another long-suffering sigh) "Fine."

And getting ready in the morning:
Me: "Come, lets comb your hair real quick and get it out of your face."
Her: "Mom.  It's fine."
Me:  "But it's in your face."
Her: "But I'm a princess.  I can just wiggle it out." (and then she demonstrates the proper princess hair tossing technique)
