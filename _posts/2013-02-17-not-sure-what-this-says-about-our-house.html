--- layout: post title: Not sure what this says about our house date: '2013-02-17T20:00:00.001-07:00' author: Lindsay tags: - mommy moments - quotes modified_time: '2013-02-17T20:00:51.899-07:00' thumbnail: http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-XsQ0q_0y18g/USGZFnONkuI/AAAAAAAABGM/CZDV9AhlpLc/s72-c/IMGP2919.JPG blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6431381294158046892.post-7081942201686057050 blogger_orig_url: http://inconclusiveexperiences.blogspot.com/2013/02/not-sure-what-this-says-about-our-house.html --- Child 1 and child 3 love to play Pet Shop.  They pretend to be animals and I pretend to buy them.  The other day they were being cats (big surprise).

Child 1: "Mom, this is a pet store.  Can you come and buy us?"

Me: "I'm right in the middle of making dinner, and I can't leave right now.  Can you wait?"

Child 1: "Can't you buy us online?"

Me: "Absolutely.  Click, click, click.  Beep, beep.  Gee, I can't wait until my new cats come in the mail!"

And they delivered themselves to my kitchen door, unpacked themselves from their remote-control crates (laundry baskets) and moved into the living room.  I got dinner made without having them underfoot and they still felt like I was playing with them.

Parenting WIN.
